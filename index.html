Frequently Asked Questions

Not sure how it works? We have compiled answers to the most common questions so that
you can easily submit your complaint/feedback

Do I have a complaint or feedback?
Yes! You can submit complaints or feedback directly through our website form. Just fill in your details and description.
Where do I select the language on complaint webpage?
You can select the language from the dropdown menu at the top-right corner of the complaint page.
What type of customer can I choose for registration?
You can choose between Individual, Business, or Distributor while registering.
How can I create a sub-distributor account?
Log in to your distributor account and navigate to "Manage Sub-Accounts" to create a sub-distributor account.
How do I activate my webpage account?
After registration, check your email for the activation link. Click it to activate your account.
